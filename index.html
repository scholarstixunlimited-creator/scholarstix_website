Who We Are

Technology That Works as Hard as You Do

Scholarstix Unlimited Inc. is a forward-thinking technology company headquartered in Meredale, Johannesburg. We innovate, design and integrate bespoke AI-powered solutions that drive operational efficiency and productivity across diverse industries in Africa and beyond.

We specialize in building AI-powered operating systems tailored specifically for African SMMEs — empowering small and medium businesses with the same technological firepower previously reserved for large enterprises.

Vertical-Specific PlatformsIndustry-aware AI built for your exact business context — not generic off-the-shelf software.

Founder & CEO
Leadership

Teboho Moloi

Founder & Chief Executive Officer — Scholarstix Unlimited Inc.

Teboho Moloi is the visionary founder behind Scholarstix Unlimited Inc., bringing a strong foundation in operational systems thinking, technology integration and strategic innovation. With a deep understanding of the African SMME landscape, Teboho identified a critical gap — that small and medium businesses were being left behind by expensive, complex enterprise software designed for large corporations.

His response was Scholarstix: a company built from the ground up to design, develop and deploy AI-powered operating systems that are practical, affordable and deeply attuned to the realities of running a business in Africa. Each of the 12 industry segments reflects Teboho's hands-on research and insight into exactly what those business owners need to thrive.

"My goal is simple — to give every African SMME owner the same operational intelligence that Fortune 500 companies take for granted. Africa doesn't need watered-down solutions. It needs systems built for Africa, by Africa."
